U.S. DEPARTMENT OF STATE Office Spokesman For Immediate Release June 2, 2009 2009/ Question Taken June 1, 2009 Daily Press Briefing Current results Texting Campaign Humanitarian Aid Pakistan Q. How much money raised texting campaign humanitarian aid Pakistan? A. The United Nations High Commissioner Refugees reported June 1, 2009, total $135,008 private donations U.S. sources humanitarian aid Pakistan received. Of total, $31,390 received via 5,722 text messages. The remainder included $84,702 received via 439 online donations; $18,916 mail major donors. # # #
